======= Ores =======

There are a lot of different ores available in zuluhotel.  Different
ores have different attributes.

Properties are as follows:
Quality: Ores with a higher quality will produce items with more
   hitpoints (durability) and weapons which deal more damage.

Base Difficulty:  The base difficulty of the ore, is added to the
difficulty of the item being crafted.

Reduced Dex Penalty: Armor crafted by certain ores will exhibit less
   of a dexterity bonus when worn by the player
